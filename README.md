# 🎓 StudentOS 
### *The AI-Powered Central Nervous System for Academic Success*

**StudentOS** is a multi-agent workspace designed to help students organize, analyze, and master their academic life. Built for the **Google Solution Challenge 2026**, it leverages Google Gemini to bridge the gap between messy study materials and high-performance learning.

---

## 🎯 UN Sustainable Development Goal
**Goal 4: Quality Education** We aim to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education by providing students—regardless of their resources—with a high-level AI tutor and organizational partner.

---

## 🚀 Key Features (Multi-Agent System)
* **Blackboard Wiz:** Multimodal vision agent that converts classroom photos into structured Markdown notes.
* **Smart Planner:** An agentic coach that syncs exam dates with a personalized study calendar.
* **Analyze Panel:** Predictive analytics to identify knowledge gaps before they become exam failures.
* **PDF Professor:** Deep document analysis for navigating complex research papers and syllabi.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** [Next.js 15](https://nextjs.org/) (App Router)
- **Styling:** [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
- **Intelligence:** [Google Gemini 1.5 Flash](https://ai.google.dev/)
- **Backend/Auth:** [Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/)
- **Deployment:** [Vercel](https://vercel.com/)
